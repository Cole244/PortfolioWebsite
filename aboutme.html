Brief Introduction

Hello and welcome to my website! I just graduated from the University of Massachusetts Amherst as an English major and member of the Commonwealth Honors College! I was awarded certificates in Creative Writing as well as Professional Writing and Technical Communication, and through my schooling I gained significant education experience in the technical writing field. As I look to break into the technical space, I've begun my professional journey by working as Folia Materials' Technical Writer!

Why Technical Writing?

Throughout high school I took multiple web page design classes which I really enjoyed! Following these courses I found the HTML/CSS that I learned to be fun to play around with and utilize for projects. This is why I initially joined the PWTC program, and I became motivated to stick with it after being exposed to all of the other skills it helps develop. Utilizing new (MadCap Flare, Visual Studio Code) and old (Word) programs to craft tech writing projects has already provided me with a wealth of experience by graduation.
